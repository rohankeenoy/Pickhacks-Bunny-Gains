Welcome

Welcome to the demo of Bunny Gains. Bunny Gains is set to be a game focused on financial well-being. A hungry rabbit must gather berries to make money. He can choose to invest in bread, which is sometimes worth more, or just sell them for cash in hopes that he will get a better price for bread later on. Moo-Money is a character that expects as many berries as possible and gives a bonus.


Financial well-being is crucial to teach at a young age, this game will exhibit the idea of risk/reward, cost/benefit, and introduces stock market concepts to all ages.
